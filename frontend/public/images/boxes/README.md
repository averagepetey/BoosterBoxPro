# Booster Box Images

Place booster box images in this directory.

## Naming Convention

Images should be named according to the set identifier:

- `op-01-blue.png` - OP-01 Romance Dawn (Blue)
- `op-01-white.png` - OP-01 Romance Dawn (White)
- `op-02.png` - OP-02 Paramount War
- `op-03.png` - OP-03 Pillars of Strength
- `op-04.png` - OP-04 Kingdoms of Intrigue
- `op-05.png` - OP-05 Awakening of the New Era
- `op-06.png` - OP-06 Wings of the Captain
- `op-07.png` - OP-07 500 Years In The Future
- `op-08.png` - OP-08 Two Legends
- `op-09.png` - OP-09 Emperors of the New World
- `op-10.png` - OP-10 Royal Blood
- `op-11.png` - OP-11 A Fist of Divine Speed
- `op-12.png` - OP-12 Legacy of the Master
- `op-13.png` - OP-13 Carrying on His Will
- `eb-01.png` - EB-01 Memorial Collection
- `eb-02.png` - EB-02 Anime 25th Collection
- `prb-01.png` - PRB-01 Premium Booster Box (The Best)

## Image Requirements

- Format: PNG or JPG
- Recommended size: 400x400px or larger (square aspect ratio works best)
- The images will be displayed in the leaderboard table avatars
